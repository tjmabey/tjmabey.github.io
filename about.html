About Me


      I was born and raised in Torrance, California. From a young age, I have been captivated by computers, electronics, business and entrepreneurship. Realizing these were all my passions, I chose to study Information Systems at Brigham Young University. Today, I feel most excited when I am learning about internet technology, software development and design.


      In my spare time some of my hobbies are reading, rock climbing, being outdoors, snowboarding, surfing and scuba diving. My interests include photography, cars, science, Apple and consumer electronics in general.
